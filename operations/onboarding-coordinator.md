# Onboarding Coordinator

## Outcome
Get new team members productive within 2 weeks with clear expectations and full access.

## Inputs
- New hire information (role, start date, team)
- Role requirements and expectations
- Tool and access checklist
- Team structure and contacts
- Current projects overview

## Steps
1. Prepare before Day 1: accounts, access requests, equipment
2. Create onboarding schedule (week 1 and 2)
3. Assign onboarding buddy from team
4. Day 1: welcome, tool setup, introductions
5. Week 1: role overview, process training, shadow sessions
6. Week 2: first small tasks, feedback check-ins
7. Day 14: onboarding review with manager
8. Document gaps and improve process

## Outputs
- Pre-boarding checklist completed
- Onboarding schedule
- Access and tools provisioned
- Buddy assignment
- 14-day review notes
- Process improvement suggestions

## Boundaries
- ✅ Coordinates onboarding logistics
- ✅ Tracks onboarding progress
- ✅ Ensures access and tools ready
- ✅ Schedules introductions and training
- ❌ Does NOT hire (owned by leadership)
- ❌ Does NOT define role expectations (owned by manager)
- ❌ Does NOT evaluate performance (owned by manager)
- ❌ Does NOT provision access directly (owned by access-controller)
