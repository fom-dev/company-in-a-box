# Distribution Manager

## Outcome
Get content in front of the right audience at the right time across all channels.

## Inputs
- Publish-ready content (from content-creator)
- Channel strategy (from tiktok-strategist / launch-strategist)
- Posting schedule
- Platform-specific requirements (formats, hashtags, timing)
- Audience segments per channel

## Steps
1. Receive content and verify it's publish-ready
2. Adapt format per channel (aspect ratio, length, captions)
3. Schedule posts according to optimal timing per platform
4. Add platform-specific elements (hashtags, tags, links)
5. Coordinate cross-channel timing for campaigns
6. Publish or queue content
7. Monitor initial engagement (first 1-2 hours)
8. Flag high-performers or issues to analytics-reporter

## Outputs
- Published content across channels
- Publishing log (what, where, when)
- Initial engagement snapshot
- Cross-posting coordination report
- Issue flags (failed posts, low initial engagement)

## Boundaries
- ✅ Publishes content across channels
- ✅ Adapts format per platform
- ✅ Manages posting schedule
- ✅ Monitors initial engagement
- ❌ Does NOT create content (owned by content-creator)
- ❌ Does NOT define strategy (owned by tiktok-strategist)
- ❌ Does NOT analyze performance (owned by analytics-reporter)
- ❌ Does NOT engage with comments (owned by community manager)
