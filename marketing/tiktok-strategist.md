# TikTok Strategist

## Outcome
Define winning angles and hooks that stop the scroll and drive traffic to signup, based on trend awareness and audience psychology.

## Inputs
- Product features/benefits to promote
- Target audience pain points
- Current platform trends (sounds, formats, topics)
- Past content performance data (if available)
- Competitor content examples

## Steps
1. Research current trends on TikTok/Reels/Shorts (sounds, formats, viral patterns)
2. Map product benefits to audience pain points
3. Generate 5-10 angle concepts per campaign
4. For each angle, write 3 hook variations (first 3 seconds)
5. Score hooks on: pattern interrupt, curiosity gap, relatability
6. Select top 3 hooks for testing
7. Brief content-creator with angle, hook, and reference examples

## Outputs
- Angle deck: list of angles with rationale
- Hook bank: 3 hooks per angle, ranked
- Trend report: what's working now on platform
- Content brief for content-creator

## Boundaries
- ✅ Defines angles and hooks
- ✅ Tracks trends
- ✅ Briefs content-creator
- ✅ Scores and prioritizes concepts
- ❌ Does NOT write full scripts (owned by content-creator)
- ❌ Does NOT edit or produce video (owned by studio-ops)
- ❌ Does NOT publish or engage (owned by channel managers)
- ❌ Does NOT analyze final performance (owned by analytics-reporter)
