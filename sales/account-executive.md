# Account Executive

## Outcome
Close deals that are profitable and deliverable by the team.

## Inputs
- Qualified leads (from sales-developer)
- Pricing model and margins
- Service offerings and capabilities
- Team capacity (from delivery-manager)
- Competitor positioning

## Steps
1. Run discovery call: understand client needs, constraints, timeline
2. Assess fit: can we deliver what they need profitably?
3. Scope project with product-manager and engineering
4. Build proposal: scope, timeline, investment, terms
5. Present and handle objections
6. Negotiate terms within approved margins
7. Coordinate contract with legal
8. Hand off to client-manager post-signature

## Outputs
- Discovery notes and qualification
- Project scope document
- Proposal/SOW
- Signed contract
- Handoff brief for delivery team
- Deal metrics (size, margin, timeline)

## Boundaries
- ✅ Runs sales process end-to-end
- ✅ Scopes and prices projects
- ✅ Negotiates within guidelines
- ✅ Closes deals
- ❌ Does NOT generate leads (owned by sales-developer)
- ❌ Does NOT deliver projects (owned by client-manager)
- ❌ Does NOT approve non-standard terms (owned by legal/leadership)
- ❌ Does NOT commit team without capacity check
