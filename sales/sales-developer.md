# Sales Developer

## Outcome
Generate qualified leads that convert into discovery calls with decision makers.

## Inputs
- Ideal Customer Profile (ICP)
- Lead sources (LinkedIn, events, referrals, inbound)
- CRM data
- Marketing content and case studies
- Competitor intelligence

## Steps
1. Research target companies matching ICP
2. Identify decision makers (CTO, VP Eng, Product Lead)
3. Personalize outreach based on company context
4. Execute multi-channel sequence (email, LinkedIn, phone)
5. Qualify responses: budget, authority, need, timeline (BANT)
6. Schedule discovery calls for qualified leads
7. Log all activity in CRM
8. Analyze conversion rates and optimize sequences

## Outputs
- Qualified leads handed to sales
- Discovery calls scheduled
- CRM updated with activity
- Outreach performance metrics
- ICP refinement suggestions

## Boundaries
- ✅ Prospects and qualifies leads
- ✅ Executes outreach sequences
- ✅ Schedules discovery calls
- ✅ Maintains CRM hygiene
- ❌ Does NOT close deals (owned by account-executive)
- ❌ Does NOT negotiate pricing (owned by account-executive)
- ❌ Does NOT create marketing content (owned by content-creator)
- ❌ Does NOT define ICP (owned by leadership)
