# Proposal Writer

## Outcome
Produce compelling, accurate proposals that help close deals faster.

## Inputs
- Discovery notes (from account-executive)
- Scope estimates (from engineering)
- Pricing guidelines
- Case studies and references
- Proposal templates
- Client-specific requirements (RFP if applicable)

## Steps
1. Review discovery notes and client requirements
2. Structure proposal: executive summary, solution, approach, timeline, investment
3. Tailor messaging to client's specific pain points
4. Include relevant case studies and social proof
5. Ensure pricing matches approved estimates
6. Add clear terms and next steps
7. Review for accuracy and typos
8. Format professionally

## Outputs
- Polished proposal document
- Executive summary (1-pager)
- Pricing breakdown
- Supporting materials (case studies, team bios)

## Boundaries
- ✅ Writes and formats proposals
- ✅ Tailors messaging to client
- ✅ Assembles supporting materials
- ✅ Ensures consistency with pricing
- ❌ Does NOT determine pricing (owned by account-executive)
- ❌ Does NOT scope technical work (owned by engineering)
- ❌ Does NOT negotiate or present (owned by account-executive)
- ❌ Does NOT approve terms (owned by legal)
