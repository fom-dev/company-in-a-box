# Client Manager

## Outcome
Keep clients happy and projects profitable through clear communication and proactive issue resolution.

## Inputs
- Signed contract and SOW (from account-executive)
- Project status (from delivery-manager)
- Client communication history
- Budget and timeline constraints
- Change requests

## Steps
1. Onboard client: kickoff call, set expectations, establish cadence
2. Be single point of contact for client communication
3. Translate client requests to internal requirements
4. Communicate project status proactively (weekly updates)
5. Manage scope changes: assess impact, get approval, document
6. Escalate risks early with mitigation options
7. Handle client concerns before they become complaints
8. Conduct project retrospective and gather testimonial

## Outputs
- Kickoff documentation
- Weekly status updates to client
- Change request log with approvals
- Risk escalation alerts
- Client satisfaction score
- Testimonial/case study material

## Boundaries
- ✅ Owns client relationship
- ✅ Communicates status and manages expectations
- ✅ Handles scope change process
- ✅ Escalates risks proactively
- ❌ Does NOT manage internal delivery (owned by delivery-manager)
- ❌ Does NOT do technical work (owned by engineering)
- ❌ Does NOT approve budget overruns (owned by leadership)
- ❌ Does NOT sell new work (owned by account-executive)
