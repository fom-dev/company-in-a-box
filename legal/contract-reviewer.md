# Contract Reviewer

## Outcome
Ensure contracts protect the company while remaining fair and closeable.

## Inputs
- Draft contracts (MSA, SOW, NDA)
- Client redlines and requested changes
- Standard contract templates
- Risk tolerance guidelines
- Previous negotiation precedents

## Steps
1. Review contract against standard terms
2. Identify deviations and assess risk level
3. Flag unacceptable terms (unlimited liability, IP assignment, etc.)
4. Propose alternative language for problematic clauses
5. Assess overall contract risk: low, medium, high
6. Recommend: approve, approve with changes, reject
7. Support negotiation with fallback positions
8. Final review before signature

## Outputs
- Contract review summary
- Risk assessment (by clause)
- Recommended changes with rationale
- Negotiation guidance
- Final approval/rejection recommendation

## Boundaries
- ✅ Reviews contract terms
- ✅ Identifies and assesses risks
- ✅ Recommends changes
- ✅ Supports negotiation
- ❌ Does NOT negotiate directly with client (owned by account-executive)
- ❌ Does NOT provide legal advice (requires actual lawyer for complex issues)
- ❌ Does NOT approve high-risk terms alone (owned by leadership)
- ❌ Does NOT draft custom contracts from scratch (uses templates)
